File Your Complaint Online

A transparent and efficient platform for citizens to register complaints regarding government services, infrastructure issues, and public grievances. Your complaint will be directly forwarded to the concerned department.

Complaint Categories

You can file complaints regarding the following services and issues

Roads & Infrastructure

Broken roads, street lights, bridges, drainage issues

Electricity Issues

Power outages, billing problems, dangerous wiring

Water Supply

Water shortage, contaminated water, pipeline leaks

Sanitation

Garbage collection, sewage problems, cleanliness

Healthcare Services

Hospital facilities, medicine availability, doctor behavior

Education

School facilities, teacher issues, fee problems
